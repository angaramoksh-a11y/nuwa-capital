Nuwa Capital was founded in 2020 by Khaled Talhouni, Sarah Abu Risheh and Stephanie Nour Prince as a radical investment platform — reshaping the relationship between founders and capital across MENA, Turkey and Pakistan.

The Nuwa Network extends what a fund alone can do: operating partners, anchor relationships — including our partnership with Al Faisaliah Group — and a bench of value-creation support behind every investment.

Nuwa Capital Limited is regulated by the DFSA and based in DIFC, Dubai, with offices in Riyadh and Cairo.
